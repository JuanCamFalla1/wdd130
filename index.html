Cochabamba

Cochabamba lies in a valley of fertile and productive land at an altitude of 2,558 meters (8,392 feet). Surrounded by cultivated fields and valuable pre-Inca and Inca remains, the city preserves its viceregal character while establishing itself as an important agricultural, manufacturing, commercial, and industrial center.
